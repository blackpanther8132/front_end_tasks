Products

  • Infer
    (Security token protocols

    Security tokens has enabled us to reimagine finance with programmability. There are few protocols out there set out to securitize real world assets that require liquidity. Security tokens helps close the gap of Irrevocably linking a smart contract to a legal document and vice versa. Infer will identify protocols which will be relevant to security tokens in the immediate future and create interoperability is, from Singularchain perspective, the most valuable addition to the eco-system.

  • InterChange
    (P2P exchange)

    Security tokens, Derivates, Options and non-fungible assets can leverage trustless and transparent environment of Blockchain. Retail investors are unable to gain a direct access to options contracts and other derivates traded on large exchange platforms and hence go through third party brokerages. Interchage’s P2P exchange will result in more transparency and can effectively remove middlemen and fee-seeking intermediaries from trading of financial assets.

  • Indenture
    (Documents on Blockchain)

    Blockchain is known for its immutability. This tamper resistance is highly effective in preventing the counterfeiting of documents and document fraud. Creating a layer of privacy over ( Zero Knowledge Proof) these documents on blockchain can enable viewing of only a select parties. Using Indenture every agreement, every process, every task, and every payment would have a digital record and signature that could be identified, validated, stored, and shared on the blockchain

  • Inscribe
    (Track and trace)

    The idea is to securely record business transactions across the supply chain. Incribe provides track-and-trace reporting, insights and analytics based on the partner’s rights on the network. The product covers full audit tail of data throughout the supply chain, real time multi party verification, cross border compliance through trusted digital signatures.

  • Incentiwise
    (Gamification)

    Smart Contracts utilize the technology to facilitate, verify, and enforce an agreement made between parties. However, suitable incentive mechanisms that can meet the diverse requirements of users in dynamic and distributed environments are still missing. Incentiwise is the gamification of tasks, incentive structures and reputation models for different environments.

  • Initiate
    (Governance)

    Organisms are biased towards their own survival. This commonly manifests in changes to the reward structure, monetary policy, or balances of power. Initiate provides on-chain coordination where one group coordinates with the other efficiently without the imbalance of power which is seen predominantly in current day organizations.
