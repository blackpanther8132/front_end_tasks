About Us

Carigar Tools was incorporated in 2018, with a vision to Innovate and leverage Experience to become the leading Power Tools brand in India. ‘Carigar’ is a word of Indian origin meaning ‘Craftsman’; our core proposition is to offer the highest quality power tools at an affordable price to the ‘Carigars’ of the country.

We have established an extensive service network throughout the country, to provide all our valued customers with thorough technical & sales support. We aim to constantly innovate and make advancements in order to best serve the needs of our customers across the country.

By partnering with the finest quality global tools manufacturers we intend to be at the forefront of developments in the power tool industry & provide our customers with the latest and quality products. We have diligently focused on establishing strong research & development capabilities to optimize our products for the requirements of our 'Carigars'.

Carigar aims to develop itself to become the industry leaders of power tools in India, by offering:

  • Partnered with state of the art manufacturing facilities, to offer the highest quality product

  • Firmly establish supply network for ‘spares’

  • We are accessible for buyers to purchase our products, both online & offline

  • Artificial Intelligence in inventory management

  • Top-of-the-line after-sales services
